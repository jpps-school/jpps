Key Facilities

Safety & Security

  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance
  • Boundary wall around campus
  • Female attendants for girls
  • First aid room

Clean Classrooms

  • Spacious rooms with natural light
  • Blackboards & bulletin boards
  • Student artwork displays
  • Ceiling fans in all rooms

Evening Tuition

  • Free for enrolled students
  • ₹300/month for external students
  • 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM (Sat-Thurs)
  • Focus on homework help

School Vans

  • Covers 12+ nearby villages
  • Transportation charge depend on distance
  • GPS tracked vehicles
  • Separate seating for boys/girls

Hostel Facility

  • Both veg and non-veg
  • ₹3000/month (including meals)
  • Evening study hours
  • Only for boys

Sanitation

  • Separate toilets for boys/girls
  • Daily cleaning schedule
  • Running water supply
  • Handwashing stations

Packed Food Canteen

  • Only sealed & packed food items available
  • Snacks approved by school administration
  • Safe, hygienic, and child-friendly choices
  • Filtered water available near canteen area

Safe Drinking Water

  • RO purified water
  • Traditional clay pot cooling
  • Separate drinking stations

Medical Care

  • Basic first aid supplies
  • Tie-up with local health center
  • Monthly health checkups

Monsoon Shelter

  • Covered verandas
  • Rain gear storage
  • Drying area for wet clothes

Parent Zone

  • Shaded waiting area
  • Drinking water access
  • Notice boards in local language

Multi-Faith Space

  • Quiet prayer corner
  • Respects all religions
  • Festival celebrations

Inverter Facility
