Innovative Teaching Methods

Activity-Based Learning

Hands-on activities using local materials to explain concepts

  • Math through farming measurements
  • Science with household items
  • Language through local stories

Multilingual Instruction

Teaching in Hindi, English, and local dialects

  • Bilingual flashcards
  • Translation exercises
  • Local language support

Story-Based Learning

Teaching concepts through engaging narratives and imagination

  • Subject topics woven into moral or adventure stories
  • Students create and share their own stories
  • Enhances memory, creativity, and comprehension

Roleplay & Simulation

Helping students understand concepts through dramatization and real-life scenarios

  • Enacting historical events or science processes
  • Mock markets or courts for practical learning
  • Character-based storytelling for language skills

Continuous Assessment System

Formative

Projects

Practical applications of concepts

Term Exams

Half-yearly and annual examinations

Progress Tracking

Individual student growth monitoring
